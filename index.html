What's going on here?

This is Conway's Game of Life -- a cellular simulation created in 1970, which has spawned a cult following and a diverse set of practical applications. You can read more about it all here.

Black makes cells alive, white makes them dead. Hit the Propagate button to advance forward and see how your cells live or die.
